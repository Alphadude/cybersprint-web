About Us

Who we are

Cybersprint Technology Service Limited (CSL) which develops Software Solutions for global Companies using leading-edge technologies. We build off -shelf Oil and Gas Applications, Single and multi-user applications, database application as well as internet or Intranet and e-commerce applications, we are also into IT consultancy and Solutions. Our Commitment to delivering value to our clients is expressed in one of the major tenets of our company philosophy: something we call “experience + intelligence=Service excellence”. Our approach to service excellence is two–fold. First, our extensive use of industry licensed standard tools and components throughout our development process enable us to provide a predictable delivery model. But our service doesn’t stop once the project is delivered. CSL continues to support your company across installations, support and maintenance, field training and upgrades processes as well. Incorporated in 2008, CSL is staffed with a team of highly skilled petroleum and gas engineers, computer Scientist, Industry consultants and Microsoft certified software developers. Based on these we remain competitive and equipped with the best tools in the industry. Our Competitive edge comes from our flexibility and abilities to develop and customize solutions on time and within budget.
